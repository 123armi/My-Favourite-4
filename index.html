About

I don't get why many people don't like reading. For me,it's the best thing in the world. Whenever you're bored, just grab a book and read. See.. It's so simple!! You can increase your vocabulary by reading. Like I usually keep a dictionary with me everytime I read a book. When you don't understand the meaning of a word just open the dictionary and search. Let me also point out a thing - Many children just look at the images of the book and done. I mean, how can they know if it's interesting or not by the pictures?? This is a little similar to the proverb "Don't Judge a book by it's cover". I'll give one example of myself. I used to hate Harry Potter, thinking what's in it. It's just some kid learning magic. Meanwhile my sister used to love it. Now I read the whole Harry Potter Series in about two months and I am addicted to it. I used to keep on reading for hours.
